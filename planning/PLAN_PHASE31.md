# Phase 31 — Blocs & Steering Committee Rework

## Status: OUTLINE ONLY — details TBD with user

This phase reworks the NPC bloc simulation and the Steering Committee (board) to make both feel more alive and consequential in Era 2.

## Themes (to be expanded)

- **Blocs as actors, not just stat containers.** By Phase 28 blocs generate diplomatic events, but they still feel passive. Phase 31 should give them clearer goals, more distinct personalities, and visible trajectories the player can read and respond to.

- **Steering Committee depth.** Board members should feel like genuine trade-offs with emerging narrative. Their ageing, retirement, and replacement should be more dramatic and player-facing.

## Open Questions (to discuss with user)

1. **Bloc goals:** Should each bloc have a visible "agenda" the player can see — e.g. East Asia is pursuing orbital infrastructure, Middle East is consolidating energy reserves? How much of this is surfaced in the UI?

2. **Bloc space activity:** In Era 2, should NPC blocs be able to reach Near Space (limited, simulated presence) — generating news items like "East Asia Orbital Station operational"? Does this create pressure or resource competition?

3. **Bloc mergers / splits:** Phase 28 implemented bloc elimination. Should mergers create new, stronger blocs? Should a declining bloc fracture into a smaller remnant?

4. **Committee events:** Should board members be able to generate proactive events — not just counters, but positive proposals? E.g. the Head of Finance proposes a bond issue.

5. **Loyalty / faction dynamics:** Could board members have factions (technocrats, internationalists, nationalists) that create internal Committee tension? Or is that too complex?

6. **Committee size:** Currently 5–7 slots. Should this scale with era (more seats in Era 2 as the organisation grows)?

## Potential Deliverables (TBD)

- Bloc agenda system (hidden or visible goals per bloc)
- Bloc Near Space presence (simulated, news-only)
- Board member proactive proposals
- Enhanced retirement/replacement narrative
- Bloc faction events (two blocs forming an alliance, pressuring the player jointly)
